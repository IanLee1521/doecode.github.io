DOE Code

Energy Science and Technology Software Center

The Energy Science and Technology Software Center, DOECode, is the centralized software management portal the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE). As a form of scientific and technical information, software is a result of the Department's research and development activities at the DOE national laboratories and other DOE facilities/contractors. This portal provides access to software packages, including source code, executable object code, and the minimum support documentation (e.g., software user guide or manual) needed to use the software or to modify the software to support subsequent development efforts.

DOECode is managed and operated by the DOE's Office of Scientific and Technical Information (OSTI).

If you have any questions or feedback please contact us by email at estsc@osti.gov
